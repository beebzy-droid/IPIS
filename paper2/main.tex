\section*{Data and code availability}
The chance-constrained RTO implementation, the DWSIM twin data, the regime-map engine, and
the figure scripts that regenerate every figure in this paper are available at
\url{github.com/beebzy-droid/IPIS}; the regime map and all figures reproduce from a single
fixed seed. The process twin is built in DWSIM \citep{dwsim} under the Peng--Robinson
equation of state \citep{peng1976}, and the model-based composition estimate it corrects is
developed in the companion paper \citep{companion2026}.
