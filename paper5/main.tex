\section*{Data and code availability}
The dynamic plant, the dynamic closed-loop orchestrator, the adaptive-conformal horizon
read-off, the two-arm and sweep experiments, and the single script that regenerates every
figure in this paper (\texttt{scripts/run\_horizon\_sweeps.py}) are available at
\url{github.com/beebzy-droid/IPIS}; all figures and the reported coverage numbers reproduce
from a single fixed seed, with the headline numbers frozen as committed evidence. The
process twin is built in DWSIM \citep{dwsim} under the Peng-Robinson equation of state
\citep{peng1976}; the steady-state response surface it corrects, the soft sensor, the
calibrated prognostics, and the per-cycle composed coverage certificate that this work lifts
to a horizon are developed in the companion papers \citep{busico_m3, busico_m1, busico_m2, busico_m4}.
