# Suggested reviewers — IECR submission (Module 4)

Submission systems ask for 3-5 suggested reviewers (name, affiliation, email) and any
opposed reviewers. Below are expertise PROFILES the manuscript needs, with candidate
names drawn from the directly relevant published literature. Before entering any name,
verify the current affiliation and email, and confirm no conflict of interest (no shared
publications, advising, or institution with the author). Names are candidates, not
endorsements.

## Profile 1 — Conformal prediction beyond exchangeability
The certificate is a physically computable instance of distribution-free coverage under
distribution shift; a reviewer from this community should check Theorem 1 and the
causal-timing argument. Candidates from the cited literature: authors of the
non-exchangeable-conformal and adaptive-conformal-inference work (e.g., R. F. Barber and
collaborators; E. J. Candès and collaborators; A. N. Angelopoulos). Verify which are
appropriate and unconflicted.

## Profile 2 — Real-time optimization / modifier adaptation
The enforcement mechanism is a convex budget added to a modifier-adaptation NLP; a
reviewer should check that the optimizer-side claims are correct and that the health
constraint is posed sensibly. Candidates: authors in the modifier-adaptation and economic
RTO lineage (e.g., the Marchetti / Chachuat / Francois modifier-adaptation line).

## Profile 3 — Prognostics and health management (process / rotating equipment)
The equipment-life guarantee rests on a calibrated remaining-life lower bound and a
degradation model anchored in bearing-fatigue and affinity-law physics; a reviewer should
check the prognostic-side modeling and the severity-to-failure mechanics. Candidates:
authors of the statistical-RUL and machinery-prognostics review literature cited in the
manuscript.

## Profile 4 (optional) — Process systems / digital twins for distillation
A debutanizer / distillation-and-control specialist can check the FUG twin, the similitude
coordinate, and the binary-key scope claim.

## Opposed reviewers
None specified. Add any direct competitors or conflicted parties here if applicable.

## Notes for entry
- ACS requires institutional emails where possible; personal/Gmail addresses are often
  rejected for reviewers.
- Aim for geographic and group diversity across the suggestions.
- The manuscript's own reference list is the best source of current, relevant names.
